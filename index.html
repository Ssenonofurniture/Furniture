Our Story

SSENONO FURNITURE CENTER is a premier manufacturer of high quality wood furniture including frames, shutters, kitchens, cabinets, doors, wardrops, beds and wardrobes using well seasoned timber.

We specialize in MDH and block board, laminated boards, solid wood like Mahogan, Musambya, Mugavu, Muvule and so on

We proudly design and develop our products to suit all income levels while maintaining exceptional quality standards.

Contact us for any furniture design and installation needs - we bring your vision to life.
